Symptoms


The first thing to be noted about the symptoms, which has been mentioned before and will be reiterated is, showing any of these symptoms does not necessarily mean you are infected. On the split side, being asymptomatic does not need to mean you are disease-free.

The most common symptoms experienced include-
  • Fever or chills
  • Dry cough
  • Shortness of breath or difficulty breathing
  • Fatigue
  • Muscle or body aches
  • Headache
  • New loss of taste or smell
  • Sore throat
  • Congestion or runny nose
  • Nausea or vomiting
  • Diarrhea


However, on the arrival of more severe symptoms like the ones below, seek immediate medical help.
  • Trouble breathing
  • Persistent pain or pressure in the chest
  • New confusion
  • Inability to wake or stay awake
  • Pale, gray, or blue-colored skin, lips, or nail beds, depending on skin tone

Stuff to do during lockdown

If you find yourself bored or have nothing much to do during quarantine at home or lockdown, here are some productive stuff to engage yourself in:
(1)Read books
Books are a great way to spend time. It is extremely engaging and a great way to expand a person's thinking and improve his/her vocabulary. (Check out various books here)
(2)Start/maintain a journal
Maintain a journal and jot down your everyday activities and experiences .


(3)Pursue new hobbies
While staying indoors with all that extra time, developing new hobbies or pursuing new hobbies is a great way to kill time. Hobbies can include:- music, dance, art, craft,etc or learning new life skills.

(5)Exercise regularly
Staying fit is extremely necessary during covid. Even if we are unable to step outside and exercise regularly. Try doing yoga at home, a ten min walk around the house, and try watching youtube tutorials for a proper workout.(Good workouts)

(5)Stay connected
Stay connected with your friends and family and keep checking on them. Give them support if required.
